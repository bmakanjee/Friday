## Feedback Notes for Juan — Next Shoot

### What worked well
1. **Vertical video format (1080x1920)** — Reels-ready, no cropping needed. Keep this up.
2. **Process variety** — 7 different videos gives us real scheduling flexibility. Excellent volume.
3. **Branded kit visible** — Jack in Monarch shirt + branded van in shots = free brand placement. Great instinct.
4. **Motivational captions on TOYOTA_001** — This format performs. The "chase excellence" angle fits the brand.

### What to improve next time
1. **We need BEFORE shots.** This is the single biggest gap. Before/after transformations are our highest-performing content format (312 likes on Ferrari 488 before/after). Without a clear "before" state, we lose the most powerful creative angle. Shoot 3-5 seconds of the car as-is before any work begins — dirty wheels, dusty dash, water spots, anything. Even if the car is relatively clean, capture the "before" state.
2. **Get a final reveal shot.** TOYOTA_004 is close (15s walkthrough), but we need a dedicated 5-10 second slow reveal — camera starts tight on a detail (badge, wheel, reflection) and pulls back to reveal the full car gleaming. This is the "money shot" for social.
3. **Audio quality.** If the motivational caption overlay is the plan, background audio doesn't matter much. But for non-captioned reels, capture natural sound — foam spraying, vacuum running, water rinsing. ASMR-style audio drives saves and shares.
4. **Client reaction / testimonial.** Even 5 seconds of the owner seeing the car for the first time = gold. "Wow" reactions are the highest-converting content format across all detailing accounts we model after. Ask the client before the shoot.
5. **Tighter detail shots.** Some photos are mid-range. We need extreme close-ups: leather grain after conditioning, wheel spoke reflections, paint clarity shots where you can see trees reflected. These perform well as carousel openers.
6. **Shoot the custom plate.** "UNRVLD 1" is a conversation starter. If it's in the video footage, great — but we need a dedicated still photo of it for carousels and stories. Personalised plates = comments and shares.
7. **Sunset/golden hour timing.** If possible, schedule final exterior shots during golden hour. Black cars look incredible in warm light, and it elevates the premium feel instantly.
