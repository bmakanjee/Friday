# JARVIS — Ads Learnings

## What works
- Video = 6x engagement vs static images (proven March 2026)
- Facebook Reels = best placement: $18.90 CPL, 54.5% of all leads (verified Mar 31)
- Facebook Stories = $12.26 CPL (small sample, 1 lead, but promising)
- Males 35-44 = golden demographic: $21.51 CPL, 45.5% of leads from 20.5% of spend (verified Mar 31)
- Consolidated Winners adset = $16.87 CPL, best adset (verified Mar 31)
- Homeowners + Property = $15.12 CPL, Car Enthusiasts = $20.48 CPL (verified Mar 31)
- G Wagon reel = best creative, 6.44% CTR in Direct Booking campaign (Mar 30)
- Best day: March 26 — 3 leads, $18.20 CPL

## What doesn't work
- Facebook Feed placement = $213.78 CPL, 40.6% of budget, only 9% of leads — TRAP
- Instagram Reels = $50.54 spent, zero leads (verified Mar 31)
- Campaign 3 (Amit's) = $59.76 CPL, adset budgets override campaign budget, spending $21/day on a $2/day budget
- Luxury Interest 17 Feb adset = $64.39 CPL, 1.65 frequency (verified Mar 31)
- Business Owners adset = $51.83 CPL, $155 spent for 3 leads
- Ages 25-34, 55-64 = zero leads combined across $113 spend
- Queensland 25-55, Golf Luxury, Yacht Owners = zero leads with $60+ spend combined

## Patterns
- CPL spikes on weekends (lower intent browsing)
- Lead form ID: 969764435579475 ("Executive Detail - $499") — CORRECT form
- Campaign 2 delivery collapses after spike days — Meta pulls back in learning phase
- Blended CPL: $47.82 (Mar 17-30), target $25. Gap: 91% above target.
- Budget pacing: $37.57/day actual vs $33/day target (13.9% over, caused by C3 overspend)

## Rules I've learned
- Always verify lead form is connected before reporting campaign health
- Never modify live campaigns without boss approval via FRIDAY
- Check adset-level budgets — campaign budget can be overridden by adset budgets
- Force FB Reels + Stories placement for lead campaigns — Feed bleeds money
- Narrow targeting to 30-50, skew male for lead form campaigns
- Campaign 3 must be paused — single biggest budget waste
